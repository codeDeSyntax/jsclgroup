JCL Group is a multi-division business focused on:

* Real Estate
* Sales of Electrical Gadgets

The company exists to solve practical needs in housing and technology with a professional, customer-focused approach.

Aim and purpose:

* Provide safe, reliable property solutions
* Supply quality and authentic electrical gadgets
* Build long-term trust with customers and partners
* Contribute to economic growth through responsible business operations

Business divisions:

## 1) Real Estate Division

Services include:

* Sale of land and houses
* Rentals of apartments and commercial spaces
* Property development projects
* Property management
* Real estate consultancy
* Land documentation assistance

Purpose:

* Provide secure and verified properties
* Offer affordable and luxury housing options
* Support customers with informed property decisions

## 2) Electrical Gadgets Division

Products include:

* Mobile phones
* Laptops and computers
* Home appliances
* Accessories such as chargers, headphones, routers, and power banks
* Smart devices such as TVs, CCTV systems, and solar gadgets

Purpose:

* Provide genuine and durable gadgets
* Offer affordable pricing
* Support modern digital living
* Provide after-sales support and guidance
